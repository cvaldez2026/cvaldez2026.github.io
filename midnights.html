Midnights (2022)

Finally, her most recent release, Midnights. This album has 13 tracks, written in 13 sleepless nights. It seemed like after the folklore - evermore fantasy, Taylor has gone back to pop. It is seen in this album that she is reminiscing her past life throughout her career. The internet broke when Taylor released her 10th album, and for the first time in history, there is not a single man in the Top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100, only Taylor Alison Swift.

The Archer's Opinions

I have to admit, at first listen I didn't like it that much. I think this is just because I was really hoping for a rock album. In the end, I am ranking it my 4th favorite Taylor Swift album.

Here are my top 5 tracks:

❤ Maroon
❤ Snow On The Beach (ft. Lana Del Rey)
❤ You're On Your Own Kid
❤ Karma
❤ Would've, Could've, Should've
