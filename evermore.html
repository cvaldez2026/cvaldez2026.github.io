evermore (2020)

Four months after the release of folklore, Taylor released its sister album evermore. It shares the same qualities as folklore, the only difference is that folklore had a wintery vibe, while evermore felt more autumn-y.

The Archer's Opinions

I'm not going to lie, I have debated once if I liked folklore or evermore more. folklore still has my heart. Either way, I think it's an amazing album, and I'm putting it on the 5th spot.
