Fearless (Taylor's Version) (2021)

Fearless is the first album Taylor Swift re-recorded. This album was initially released in 2008 under the Big Machine Records. Because she wanted full ownership of her songs, she is re-recording them and calls them "Taylor's Version" As she releases the re-recorded albums, she also includes vault tracks, this album has six. The first song she ever re-recorded was Love Story and it was so heartwarming to hear adult Taylor sing it again. The first line of the song is "We were both young when I first saw you," and it just felt like Taylor Swift was talking to her fans, considering the 13 year difference between when it was first released and the re-recording.
