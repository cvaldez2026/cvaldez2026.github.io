Red (Taylor's Version) (2021)

After Fearless, Taylor Swift released the re-recording of Red> on the same year. It has 9 vault tracks, one of which is a 10 minute version of an already existing song, All Too Well. What makes the album special is that it has hints of pop, rock, and country, all at the same time. Red is considered to be her most heartbreaking album, but Swift also closing the album with the song Begin Again, showing that after a troubling, toxic relationship, it can all turn right back around once you find someone that would treat you better. It doesn't have to be a partner, it could just be yourself.
