reputation (2017)

Reputation is the last album Taylor Swift released under Big Machine Records. The album revolved around the hate, rumors, and gossip that spread about her and how despite the hardships, she found the love of her life, Joe Alwyn. During this era, Taylor was quite literally a ghost. Nobody saw her in months, she did zero interviews, but tweeted 13 times. When the album dropped, she claimed in Call It What You Want that she was doing better than she ever was. A reputation Stadium Tour documentary was also released on Netflix.
