1989 (2014)

Taylor Swift's 5th studio album is named after the year she was bron, 1989. This is where her music career really took a turn and went all out popstar. 1989 is the most awarded pop album of all time, receiving 158 awards. This includes the Album of the Year award in the 58th Grammy Awards in 2016. This album is all about how Taylor enjoys her new found life with friends and in New York, and just shaking off the hate.
