folklore (2020)

Taylor Swift released folklore in the midst of quarantine. She is once again experimenting with other genres, such as indie, folk, and alternative. The whole album was very poetic and it truly shows Taylor's talents as a lyricist. This album received so much love and eventually won ALbum of the Year in the 63rd Grammy Awards in 2021. Taylor also performed it live in a Disney+ documentary concert, "Folklore: The Long Pond Studio Sessions."
