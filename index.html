Who is Taylor Swift

Global super star, Taylor Alison Swift, has been in the music industry since 2006. She began with writing country music then slowly explored other genres. Swift is considered one of, if not, the best singer-songwriter of our generation, I am surprised you are even reading this paragraph. Anyway, in this website, I, The Archer, will guide you through all of Taylor's eras. Just a note, most of the text that will appear in this guide are highly based on my opinions.

The Number 13

Taylor Swift considers 13 as her lucky number for many reasons. Her first reason being she was born on the 13th of December, 1989. It also has great significance in her career, which she explained once in an interview.

"Basically, whenever a 13 comes up in my life, it's a good thing."
