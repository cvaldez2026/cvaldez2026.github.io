Lover (2019)

Lover is the first album Taylor Swift released after parting ways with her former record label, Big Machine Records. What makes this album special is you can really see Taylor's maturirty. Like how she forgot all the hate existed, she didn't feel love nor hate, just indifference, or how she once believed love would be burning red, but it's golden like daylight. This album was a huge step in her career, her love life, and even in politics. Netflix released another documentary, titled "Miss Americana," in which Taylor talked about what it's like to be a woman in the music industry and in the public.
