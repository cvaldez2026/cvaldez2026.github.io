Speak Now (2010)

Taylor Swift's third studio album, Speak Now, was where you'd start noticing her shifting genres. Speak Now is a rock-pop album, the only country track there was Mean, which won Best Country Song and Best Country Solo Performance in the 54th Grammy Awards in 2012.

The Archer's Opinions

My favorite album of all time. Absolutely nothing compares to Speak Now. It is rumored to be the next released re-recording and I am the most excited. This was the album that introduced me to Taylor Swift and made me fall in love with her, and music in general.
