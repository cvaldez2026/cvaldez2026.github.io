Taylor Swift (2006)

Taylor Swift is Taylor's debut country album. It was released by Big Machine Records on the 24th of October, 2006. With five singles on the album, Tim Mcgraw, Teardrops on My Guitar, Our Song, Picture to Burn and Should've Said No, Swift was able to top many charts. She was then awarded Best New Artist during the 50th Grammy Awards in 2008. This was the beginning of Taylor Swift owning the music industry.
